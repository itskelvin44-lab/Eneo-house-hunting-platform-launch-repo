Why Direct Contact Beats Middlemen Every Time

April 27, 2026 — Eneo Team

Most rental platforms make you chat through their app. Some charge agents a commission. Others hide phone numbers behind paywalls. Eneo does the opposite — and here's why.

No Chat APIs. No WhatsApp Bots. No Hidden Fees.

When you book a property on Eneo, you don't get a chat window. You get the landlord's actual phone number. Tap "Call Now" and you're speaking to a real person. Tap "Send SMS" and your message goes straight to their inbox. No middleman. No per-message fees. No "message template approvals." Just direct communication.

Why This Matters for Tenants

A phone call tells you things text can't. Is the landlord friendly? Do they sound professional? Can they answer your questions on the spot? A 2-minute call can replace 20 minutes of back-and-forth texting.

Why This Matters for Landlords

You don't need to install a separate app. You don't need to monitor a chat inbox. Your phone rings, you answer, and you arrange the viewing on the spot. It's the simplest possible way to connect with tenants.

No Agents, No Commissions

Eneo doesn't take a cut of your rent. Listings are free. Bookings are free. The landlord keeps 100% of the rent. The tenant pays no booking fees. Everyone wins.
