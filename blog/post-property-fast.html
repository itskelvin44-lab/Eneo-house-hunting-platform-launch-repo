How to Post a Property That Gets Booked Fast

April 27, 2026 — Eneo Team

You've got a property to rent. You want it filled — and fast. Here's how to create an Eneo listing that tenants can't scroll past.

1. Use Clear, Recent Photos

Eneo compresses your photos automatically to around 200KB each — so they load fast without killing quality. Upload up to 10 photos per listing. The first photo becomes your cover image, so make it count. Show the living room, bedroom, kitchen, bathroom, and any outdoor space. Skip the filters — tenants want to see the real thing.

2. Capture Your GPS Location Accurately

Eneo lets you capture your location with one tap — just stand at the property and use GPS. Or enter coordinates manually from Google Maps. An accurate pin means tenants can verify your location instantly with "Open in Google Maps."

3. Write a Detailed Description

Don't just say "2BR apartment." Tell tenants about nearby landmarks, security features, parking availability, water supply, and internet access. The more details, the fewer questions you'll need to answer later.

4. Price It Right

Check what similar properties in your area are listed for. A competitively priced listing gets more views, more saves, and more bookings. Eneo shows the price prominently on every card — make sure yours stands out.

5. Respond Quickly

⚠️ Remember Eneo's 3-Day Rule: If a tenant books your property and you don't contact them within 3 days, the listing is automatically removed. Fast responses keep your listing active and your reputation strong.

That's it. Fill in the details, add your photos, capture your location, and post. Eneo does the rest — no fees, no agents, no hidden costs.
